02 — The Vision

FUND: Community Infrastructure
for the Agent Economy on Base

Not a meme. A platform. A place where crypto communities grow without hiring a full team, and where AI agents earn, hold, and transact with full on-chain transparency.

The Core Thesis

Every growing crypto community faces the same bottleneck: human attention doesn't scale. Someone has to monitor social channels at 2am. Someone has to write the weekly update. Someone has to track on-chain metrics and alert the community. Someone has to find new partners.

Most small-cap teams can't hire for this. They end up with burnout, inconsistency, or expensive agencies that don't understand the project.

Fundiora Agent Infrastructure is the alternative: a suite of specialized AI agents that handle the operational work — monitoring, alerting, content, analysis — at a fraction of the cost of a human team, running 24/7 without fatigue.

And because these agents operate on-chain, using FUND as the payment and coordination token, every transaction creates demand. Every agent service strengthens the token's utility layer. The community grows. The agents work. The token gets used.

🎯 Transparency First

On-chain agents, public dashboards, verifiable state. No black boxes.

🔧 Utility Over Hype

FUND's value comes from real agent services, not speculation narratives.

🌱 Community Owned

Yield, agents, and governance all flow back to FUND holders.

🔒 Sustainable Model

Agent fees + treasury = self-funding development, indefinitely.

6 Agents.
1 Infrastructure Layer.

Each agent is specialized, measurable, and built to solve a specific operational problem. Together they form the operational backbone of Fundiora — and the foundation of the platform play.

CommunityPulse
Sentiment & Social Intelligence
Monitors Twitter, Telegram, and Discord for Fundiora mentions in real time. Tracks sentiment trends, flags negative discussions before they spread, and surfaces the topics the community cares about most.
Weekly Outputs
  • Sentiment score (0–100, updated daily)
  • Alert when sentiment drops >15%
  • Community health report (Fridays)
📊
YieldVault
Treasury & Rewards
Tracks on-chain FUND holder eligibility for the 5% yearly yield program. Calculates rewards based on held amount and holding duration. Feeds the dashboard in real time.
Weekly Outputs
  • Live yield eligibility tracker
  • Quarterly reward calculations
  • Treasury balance on-chain report
🚨
AlertBeacon
On-Chain Event Monitoring
Watches the FUND contract and Uniswap V3 LP pool for significant events: large transfers, new holder spikes, liquidity changes, unusual trading activity, and wallet movements.
Weekly Outputs
  • Telegram alerts for txns >$500
  • New holder spike notifications
  • Pool event log (daily digest)
✍️
ContentHub
Content & Publishing
Manages the social content calendar across Twitter and Telegram. Generates post ideas, drafts content, maintains consistent scheduling, and tracks engagement metrics per post.
Weekly Outputs
  • 12-week rolling content calendar
  • Auto-generated post drafts (3/week)
  • Weekly engagement report
💼
TreasuryMind
Finance & Transparency
Monitors the Fundiora treasury wallet on-chain. Tracks all inflows (transaction tax, fees) and outflows (yield payments, development costs). Publishes transparent monthly financial reports.
Weekly Outputs
  • Live treasury dashboard feed
  • Monthly financial report
  • Yield sustainability projections

Partnerships & Growth
