6 Agents.
1 Infrastructure Layer.

Each agent is specialized, measurable, and built to solve a specific problem. Together they form the operational backbone of Fundiora.

CommunityPulse
Sentiment & Social
Monitors Twitter, Telegram, and Discord for Fundiora mentions in real time. Tracks sentiment trends, flags negative discussions, and surfaces community hot topics before they escalate.
Outputs
  • Daily sentiment score (0–100)
  • Alert when sentiment drops >15%
  • Weekly community health report
📊
YieldVault
Treasury & Rewards
Tracks on-chain holder eligibility for the 5% yearly yield program. Calculates rewards based on held amount and duration. Feeds the dashboard in real time.
Outputs
  • Live yield eligibility tracker
  • Quarterly reward calculations
  • Treasury balance reports
🚨
AlertBeacon
On-Chain Monitoring
Watches the FUND contract and LP pool for significant events: large transfers, new holder spikes, liquidity changes, unusual trading activity.
Outputs
  • Telegram alerts for large txns (>$500)
  • New holder notifications
  • Pool event log
✍️
ContentHub
Content & Posting
Manages the social content calendar. Generates post ideas based on community topics, tracks engagement, and maintains a consistent posting schedule across Twitter and Telegram.
Outputs
  • 12-week content calendar
  • Weekly engagement report
  • Auto-generated post drafts
💼
TreasuryMind
Finance & Transparency
Monitors the Fundiora treasury wallet on-chain. Tracks all inflows (tax, fees, rewards) and outflows (yield payments, development). Publishes transparent monthly reports.
Outputs
  • Live treasury dashboard
  • Monthly financial reports
  • Yield sustainability model
🚀
GrowthGrid
Partnerships & Growth
Scans the Base ecosystem for potential partners — new token launches, protocol integrations, influencer collaborations. Qualifies opportunities and drafts outreach.
Outputs

How it works: Holders with 100K+ FUND earn 5% annually, capped at 10M FUND per wallet. Rewards are calculated on-chain and paid out manually from the treasury every quarter.

Sustainability Model

Yield is funded by a 2% transaction tax and the project treasury. At current market cap (~$24K), the program is small but manageable.

As the market cap grows to $500K+, the treasury generates enough yield income to sustain the program indefinitely — without minting new tokens.
